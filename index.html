Turn an orange into a candle

Yes, this works

Needed: an orange, a lighter, a knife, a half orangeful of vegetable oil

Score the orange peel, twist and pull the top half peel off the fruit and bottom half.

Carefully take out the fruit from the bottom half, making sure to leave the stem attached, works much better with big oranges.

Pour vegetable into the bottom half peel, over the stem, making it absorb as much oil as possible.

Light the stem patiently, could take a minute before flame gets attached.

Icecream bread

Also works a treat

Directions:

Put oven on to 200 degrees (c). Empty tub of icecream into a bowl. Add self-raising flour, until looks like cake batter. Put in baking cake pan, which you should coat with butter (use small bit of greaseproof paper to wipe it on). Put in oven for 25 minutes, check it with toothpick (should poke in and come out clean - no goo). Let it cool, about half an hour.

You can use whatever flavour icecream you want.
